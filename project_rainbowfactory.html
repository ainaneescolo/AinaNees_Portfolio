Rainbow Factory

  • Gameplay Programmer
  • 5
  • 6 Weeks
  • Unity Engine

A cooperative game where each player will be responsible for different tasks, together they will have to coordinate to paint and deliver packages to different continents. In addition, a vigilant robot will be trying to prevent the delivery of any package. The educational part of our game is found by mixing and using primary colors to get another variety of colors. Another educational aspect are the countries and continents, the packages arrive with a flag of a country and the player will have to place it at the sending point of the correct continent. The serious aspect is to deliver the packages, to differentiate the different delivery points by continents.
