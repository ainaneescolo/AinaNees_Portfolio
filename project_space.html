The game takes place in a planetary system. It is threatened by 3 types of meteorites, which each damage more or less than others.
Playing from the role of the sun your objective is to protect the planets from the meteorites, to carry out this task you will have
to manipulate the speed of your planets (Fast - Normal - Slow) and prevent the meteorites from hitting the planets by making them reach the sun.


  • Role: Gameplay Programmer

  • Team Size: 2

  • Time frame: 4 Weeks

  • Engine: Unity Engine
