Grandmaster

  • 1
  • 4 Weeks
  • Unity Engine

Grandmaster takes place on a chessboard.
The goal is to win in a chess game in which both you and your opponent can use skills. These skills can be acquired through a store, you will receive coins for each tile removed from the opponent, each female tile a different value to coins. You can access various skills, such as having two consecutive turns with the same tile or being able to change the movements of one tile for another. When put into use these will be assigned to a particular tile. The durability of the abilities is limited and depends on the skill. As any chess game to win you will have to eliminate the king of your opponent; likewise, if your king is eliminated you lose the game.
