Firefly

Github


You are a firefly and your mission is to purify all the inhabitants in order to calm your brother, the cause of chaos.
Everything you touch will be purified and turn bright white, both flora and fauna, but beware of the fauna,
they are in a corrupt state and will want to attack you.
The game has different possible endings depending on your actions while playing.
Each endig comes with an animation to further explain the story

  • Role: Gameplay Programmer

  • Team Size: 2

  • Time frame: 4 Weeks

  • Engine: Unity Engine

Firefly is a 2D platform game in horizontal orientation. It is a game of point A to point B. To make a more interesting
product we focus on improving it visually with animations and effects and adding elements like the different endings to
provide an element of replayability.
In the game there is a local game save system, which saves both your number of lives and position as plants and
enemies that you have already purified. There are different mechanics and behaviors of enemies, in addition to an extensive level design.
